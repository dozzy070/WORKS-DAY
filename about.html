Our History

We started our journey with a mission to help businesses succeed through innovation and dedication. **Founded: 2008 **Headquarters:** Lagos, Nigeria **Industry:** Renewable Energy & Technology Solutions
2008 The Beginning GreenTech Innovations was founded in Lagos by three university graduates passionate about clean energy. With just ₦500,000 pooled together, the team started in a small rented office, focusing on solar panel installations for households.
2010 First Breakthrough The company secured its first government contract to supply solar-powered streetlights to rural communities. This marked GreenTech’s entry into large-scale renewable energy projects.
2013 Expansion & Recognition GreenTech opened new branches in Abuja and Port Harcourt. The company won the **National Energy Innovation Award**, recognizing its role in promoting sustainable solutions across Nigeria.
2016 Technological Leap The firm launched its **SmartSolar™ System**, a hybrid energy solution combining solar panels with IoT-based monitoring. This innovation positioned GreenTech as a leading tech-driven energy company.
2019 Global Partnerships GreenTech partnered with German and Chinese manufacturers to improve product efficiency and reduce costs. The partnership allowed them to expand into Ghana and Kenya.
2021 Sustainability Commitment The company pledged to achieve **carbon neutrality by 2035**. It also established the **GreenTech Foundation**, focusing on providing free solar solutions to schools and healthcare centers in underserved areas.
2023 Present Day GreenTech Innovations is now a **pan-African leader in renewable energy**, employing over 2,000 staff and powering more than **1 million homes and businesses**. The company continues to invest in research, battery technology, and clean energy infrastructure.
